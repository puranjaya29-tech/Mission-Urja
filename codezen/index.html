Why We Started Mission Urja

At StarTech, we believe that a sustainable future starts with awareness and action. Mission Urja was created to educate and empower individuals about energy conservation, helping them track their consumption and adopt greener lifestyles. Together, we can make a significant impact on the environment and create a cleaner, more energy-efficient world.

What This Website Contains

  • Energy Calculator: Helps users calculate their energy consumption and find ways to save power.
  • Tips Section: Provides useful tips on how to reduce energy waste and increase efficiency.
  • Leaderboard: Displays the top users who have saved the most energy, motivating others to compete.
  • Articles: Features expert-written articles on energy conservation and green living.
  • Contact Us: Allows users to reach out for support, feedback, or collaboration opportunities.

Contest and Prizes

Mission Urja hosts an exciting competition every three months, encouraging users to reduce their energy consumption and adopt eco-friendly practices. Participants are ranked on a leaderboard based on their energy-saving contributions.

The top 10 users on the leaderboard will receive exclusive prizes, including:

  • First Prize: ₹10,000 and a Certificate of Excellence
  • Second Prize: ₹7,500 and a Certificate of Achievement
  • Third Prize: ₹5,000 and a Certificate of Recognition
  • Fourth to Tenth Prize: ₹2,000 each and Certificates of Participation

Join the challenge, save energy, and win amazing rewards!
